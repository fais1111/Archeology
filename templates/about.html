About Sri Lanka 3D Model Viewer

Project Overview

The Sri Lanka 3D Model Viewer is a web-based platform for viewing, managing, and sharing 3D models related to Sri Lankan architecture, landmarks, and cultural artifacts.

Features

Upload and view 3D models in various formats including OBJ, GLTF, GLB, and FBX. Browse models in an interactive gallery and manage your collection with ease.

Supported Formats

OBJ, GLTF, GLB, FBX, DAE, 3DS

Technology

Built with Flask, Three.js, and modern web technologies to provide a seamless 3D viewing experience in your browser.

Purpose

This platform aims to preserve and showcase Sri Lankan heritage through digital 3D models, making cultural artifacts accessible to researchers, students, and the general public.
